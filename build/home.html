The only dog friendly shopping centre on the Gold Coast.

Q Super Centre is a sub-regional community based convenient shopping centre prominently located in Mermaid Waters on the Gold Coast.

Q Super Centre offers a unique retail combination with 3 major supermarkets; Woolworths, Coles Superstore and ALDI, complemented by an alfresco dining precinct with 13 dining choices including The Coffee Club, Fast Wok, Tokyo Kitchen, 88 Thai, Jamroc Jamaican Jerk Chicken, Leoleos Seafood as well as Goldstein’s Bakery, Noodle Box, Subway and McDonald’s. To see all of the retailers we have to offer, visit our interactive centre directory.
